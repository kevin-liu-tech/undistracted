The Problem

Distracted Driving is a problem all over the world. Almost 1.35 million people die every year in distracted driving accidents, and it's not going down. This needs to be fixed but how? This is where our company, Undistracted comes in.

Our Solution

Every second, a sidemounted car camera takes a picture of the driver. This image is then fed into our software. We use a Convalutional Neural Network (CNN) to analyze this image. If we detect a driver thst is distracted, an alert is sent to the driver and will remind them to focus. We will also send weekly progress reports to your email!

How Well Does it Work?

Our accuracy with our model is above 99% both on training and test data. We even created our own data, and we still had a 99%+ accuracy rate. Suffice to say, you don’t have to worry about any false alarms!

Frequently Asked Questions

Will My Car Get Damaged?

This is a question we get asked a lot, and we’re happy to say, that we haven’t caused any damages. Our cameras are rated to last for 7 years, and they’re designed in a way where they can easily be removed but stay sturdy at the same time. Since they’re also external, they’re much easier to manage and check on!

Why do we use AI?

There are so many factors that change which make it really hard for a conventional algorithm to properly deduce what’s happening. AI and Machine Learning allows us to completely bypass this limitation. We trained our model on 10 or 100 images, we’ve used 2 million images, resulting in an ultra-high accuracy rate.

How will this benefit me?

Other than the obvious saving lives, how else does this product help you? Well, we’ve teamed up with TD, and we’ve gotten a permit to reduce insurance premiums. This basically means that if you use our product, and you give your driving results to your insurance provider, you can save as much as 25% on insurance a month.

What makes us different?

For the longest time, our competitors have been unable to scale simply due to high prices, and unnecessary fees. Furthermore, almost all previous attempts have been focused on the commercial transportation industry. There are over 1.2 billion cars on the road today, which is a huge untapped market. Many of which hope to save on their insurance premiums, which can be done with our solution!

How much does this cost?

It's absolutely free to use our service. We've paired up with our insurance providers to mail out free bluetooth cameras that can be installed in your car. It's simple, all you need to do is sign up and download the app, install webcam, and start driving!

Privacy issues?

This is arguably the most important one to some people, and our competitors have suffered from this in the past as well. We don’t keep any of the pictures we take. Those pictures are only stored on your phone and are deleted after 7 days. This is to make sure that your private information doesn't fall into the wrong hands.
